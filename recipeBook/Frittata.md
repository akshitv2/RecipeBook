# Fritatta

## Recipe
1. Add around 6 eggs in mixing bowl
2. Add 80 mL / 1/3 cup heavy cream whisk slowly careful to not introduce bubbles
3. Add chopped onion in olive oil till caramelize (maillard) then add garlic minced.
4. add some veggies, like zucchini, potato, capsicum etc thinner slices.
5. stir the oil pan can add green leafs a little later
6. Add cheese on veggies
7. Add egg mix on top
8. in the pan cook till the edge sets then move to oven sprinkle with parsley/basil
