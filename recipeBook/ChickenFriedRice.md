# Chicken Fried Rice
![img.png](img.png)

## Ingredients
1. 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old; jasmine works best)
2. 1 cup chicken, diced or shredded
3. 2 eggs, lightly beaten
4. 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
5. 3 tablespoons oil (vegetable, canola, or sesame)
6. 3–4 green onions, sliced
7. 1 small onion, diced (optional)
8. 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
9. Soy Sauce
10. Schezwan Sauce (if needed)
11. White vinegar

## Recipe
1. Cook diced chicken seperately ideally with ginger garlic paste
2. Start out with eggs beaten and cooked into a bhurji
3. Seperately add oil and cook your veggies with mild seasoning
4. Add cooked rice next with oil to fry it along with veggies
5. Add a dash of soy sauce and vinegar
6. Add cooked chicken on top and let it fry well
