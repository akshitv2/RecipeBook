# Pancakes
![recipeBook/images/Pancake.png](recipeBook/images/Pancake.png)
## Ingredients
1. 1 Egg
2. 1/4 Measuring Cup Sugar
3. 1/2 Measuring Cup Milk
4. 1.5 Tbsp Oil
5. 1.5 Tbsp Oil
6. 1 Large Cup All Purpose Flour (Maida)
7. Baking Powder 1Tbsp

## Recipe
8. Whisk all together until thick and foamy
9. Put on Flat pan and heat until bubbly
10. Flip
