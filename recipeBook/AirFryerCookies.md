# Air Fryer Cookies

## Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter 
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar 
- 1/4 cup brown sugar 
- A dash of vanilla extract 
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour 
- 1/4 tablespoon baking soda 
- 1/4 tablespoon salt 
- Chocolate chips, walnuts, or raisins (optional)

## Recipe
1. Add half a cup of unsalted butter (ideally) to a mixing bowl
2. Add 1/4 cup granulated Sugar
3. Add 1/4 cup brown sugar
4. Mix using a mixer thoroughly until cloudy
5. Add a dash of vanilla extract
6. 1 1/2 Cup of all purpose flour
7. 1/4 Tbsp baking soda
8. 1/4 Tbsp Salt
9. Mix again thoroughly
10. Add choco chips/walnuts/raisins (optional)
11. Take out small balls of dough and place on parchment paper on air fryer
12. Preheat if needed, heat for around 15 mins at 150'C
13. Let them cool for 5 mins and harden
14. Enjoy!
